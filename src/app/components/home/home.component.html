Welcome to Ascend

Daily Review Feature

The Daily Review feature allows you to track your daily wellness ratings and habits, as well as record guided journal entries to reflect on your day. Simply select a rating from 1-10 for each category, mark your habits as completed or not, and enter your journal entries into the provided fields.

Reports Feature

The Reports feature allows you to view your progress over time by displaying a bar graph of your wellness ratings from 1-10 for a selected range of dates. Scroll through the dates and select a date to review your Daily Review entries from that day.
